Our Services

Let our Professional services help you...

Let us help you get back to what you do best... And that is running your business! Our Professional service, located in Seeduwa-Katunayake, Sri Lanka, is ideal for any small and medium business owner. If your business has grown to the point where you no longer have time to handle your :

  • Taxes (Income Tax, VAT, SVAT,NBT,ESC…etc)
  • Outsourcing-Bookkeeping & Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Company Secretarial work ( Company incorporation, Annual Return…etc)
  • BOI Projects

or you need assistance with your:

  • Payroll
  • Projects Reports
  • Fraud Investigation & Dispute consolation Procedures
  • Physical Assets Verification

  • Company Profile

And those are just a few of the services that we offer!

We're not limited to just Seeduwa-Katunayak either! We can travel to your office or you can travel to ours...whatever is most convenient for you. We can remotely connect to your computer. It's as easy as that!
